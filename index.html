Privacy & Data Protection

🛡️

Your Privacy is Our Priority

MindGuard uses cutting-edge privacy technology to keep your mental health data completely secure and anonymous.

🔒 Differential Privacy

Your individual data is mathematically protected by adding carefully calibrated noise, making it impossible to identify specific users while preserving overall trends for research.

🏠 Federated Learning

Machine learning happens on your device, not our servers. Only aggregated, anonymous insights are shared to improve the early warning system.

🚫 No Personal Identifiers

We don't collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or any information that could identify you. Your data is linked only to a random, anonymous ID.

📱 Local Processing

Mood analysis and early warnings are generated on your device. Your journal entries and personal thoughts never leave your phone.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Parental Dashboard

If you opt in, parents see only high-level trends (like "mood has been stable this week"). They never see journal entries or specific mood logs.

⚖️ COPPA Compliance

We fully comply with children's privacy laws. Users under 16 need parental consent, and we collect minimal data necessary for the app to function.
